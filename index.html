Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.

How do I purchase images on PicturePlanet?

You can purchase images using credits or through a subscription plan. Credits allow you to download files as needed, while subscriptions provide a set number of downloads per month. Visit our pricing page for more details.

What types of licenses are available?

We offer standard and extended licenses. The standard license covers most uses, including advertising and web usage, while the extended license is for higher volume reproduction and resale of products. For more information, see our license details.

How do I download my purchased images?

After purchasing, go to your 'Downloads' page under your account. From there, you can access and download all files associated with your purchases.

How can I update my account information?

To update your personal details, sign in to your account, navigate to your profile, and select 'Account Settings'. Here, you can update your email, password, and other personal information.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the sign-in page and follow the instructions to reset your password. An email with reset instructions will be sent to your registered email address.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

Refunds are available under certain conditions. If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please contact our customer service team with your order details. More information can be found in our refund policy.
